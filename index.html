About

I am a first-year PhD student at Syracuse University in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, working in the Dynamic Locomotion and Robotics (DLAR) Lab, advised by Prof. Zhenyu Gan. I received my Master's degree in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from Syracuse University.

My research interests span Robotics and AI, with a focus on legged robots, humanoid robots, mechanical design, and AI-driven locomotion control. I am also interested in electronics design, Finite Element modeling, and robotic exoskeletons.
